Project Idea

ReviewOU is an OU-specific review and Q&A platform designed to give students clear, detailed, and centralized information about classes, professors, housing, and campus life. The site brings together reivews, ratings, and a community question board so students can get honest feedback all in one place. By organizing everything on a simple platform, ReviewOU helps students make better decisions without having to search multiple websites.

Problem for Campus Students

Students at OU struggle to find reliable and detailed information about professors, classes, housing, and campus resources because the current options are scattered, limited, or outdated. Most students rely on word of mouth or generic sites like RateMyProfessor and Google reviews, which don't provide enough detail for real decision making. This becomes a problem especially during enrollment, housing searches, and when students need honest campus advice but don't know where to look.

Your Proposed Solution

ReviewOU solves this problem by putting all OU-specific reviews and cmapus information in one place. The site includes professor and class ratings, housing reviews, campus like feedback, a Q&A board, and tools like search and filters to help students find exactly what they need. By keeping everything centralized and letting students post with their OU login, the platform becomes more reliable, easier to use, and more helpful for making decisions about classes, housing, and everyday campus life.

Key Features

  • Professor and class ratings that let students share detailed reviews and experiences.
  • Dorm and apartment ratings with filters for price, distance, and amenities.
  • Campus life reviews to help students learn about difference parts of OU living.
  • Q&A board inspired by Reddit/OU Moms Facebook page where students can ask questions and get honest feedback.
  • Search and filter tools plus a map with pinpointed places across campus to help students quickly find information they need.

Target Users

The main users of ReviewOU are OU undergraduate students, incoming freshmen, transfer students, and international students who need reliable information about campus life. They will use the site to search reviews, apply filters, read detailed feedback, and post their own reviews or questions through the Q&A board.
