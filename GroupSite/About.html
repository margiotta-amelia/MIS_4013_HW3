ReviewOU

Our team is developing ReviewOU, a website where OU students can find reliable and detailed reviews about professors, classes, dorms, apartments, and campus life. The goal is to put all this information in one place so students don't have to rely on random websites or word of mouth. ReviewOU also includes a Q&A board and easy-to-use filters to help students quickly find what they need.
