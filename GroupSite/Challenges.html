Project Challenges & Potential Solutions

This page outlines some of the main challenges we expected to face while creating ReviewOU. These hurdles helped us think ahead about what might limit our design and what adjustments we would need to make along the way.

Limited Initial Reviews

Description: Collecting enough early reviews were difficult since the platform depends on student contributions.

Impact: A lack of reivews could make the site feel incomplete or less useful to first-time users.

Potential Solution: Pull publicly available reviews and encourage students to post by promoting the site in student groups.

Maintaining Verified and Honest Feedback

Description: Ensuring reviews are genuine can be challenging even with OU 4x4 login requirements.

Impact: Unverified or biased reviews could reduct trust in the platform.

Potential Solution: Add reporting tools and simple moderation to remove inappropriate or inaccurate content.

Interactive Map Limitations on Google Sites

Description: We planned an interactive map to help students find important places, but Google Sites doens't support the features we needed.

Impact: Without an interactive map, students lose an easy way to visually explore campus spots and landmarks.

Potential Solution: As an alternative, we highlighted essential campus buildings and locations that freshmen and transfer students should know.
