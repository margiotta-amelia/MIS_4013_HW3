Why This Project Matters to Students

The idea is important because OU students often can't find clear, detailed, and reliable information about professors, housing, and campus life in one place. ReviewOU brings everything together so students don't have to rely on scattered websites or word of mouth. Having a centralized platform helps students make better deicisons and feel more confident about their choices at OU.

Centralized Information Source

Students no longer have to search multiple websites or rely on word of mouth to find reliable reviews.

Better decision-making

Detailed ratings and feedback help students choose classes, housing, and campus resources with more confidence.

Stronger student community

The Q&A board and reviews let students share real experiences and help each other navigate OU.

Examples & Scenarios

A first-year student is trying to decide between two professors for a required class. By reading detailed reviews on ReviewOU, they learn that one professor is known for being engaging and fair, while the other has a reputation for being disorganized. This information helps the student choose the professor that best fits their learning style.

  • A transfer student learning about campus life
